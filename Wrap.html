A chicken wrap is a delicious and easy meal made with cooked chicken, veggies, and a tasty sauce, all wrapped in a soft tortilla.

  • Chicken breast (or thighs) 1-2 pieces
  • Tortilla wraps 2-4 pieces
  • Lettuce - a few leaves
  • Tomato - 1, sliced
  • Cucumber - a few slices
  • Cheese (cheddar or your choice) - a few slices or shredded
  • Mayonnaise - 2 tablespoons
  • BBQ sauce or ranch dressing - 2 tablespoons
  • Olive oil - 1 tablespoon
  • Salt and pepper - to taste
  1. Cook the Chicken:
    • Heat olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
    • Season chicken with salt and pepper.
    • Cook chicken for about 6-7 minutes on each side, until fully cooked.
    • Remove from the pan and slice into strips.
  2. Prepare the Wrap
    • Lay a tortilla on a flat surface.
    • Spread mayonnaise and BBQ sauce (or ranch) on the tortilla.
  3. Add the Chicken
    • Arrange the sliced chicken over the veggies
  4. Wrap it Up
    • Fold the sides of the tortilla inwards and roll it up tightly.
