Fried Chicken

  • Fried chicken tenders (4-6 pieces, store-bought or homemade)
  • Tortilla wraps (2-4 pieces)
  • Lettuce (a few leaves)
  • Tomato (1, sliced)
  • Cucumber (a few slices)
  • Cheese (cheddar, mozzarella, or your choice) â€“ a few slices or shredded
  • Mayonnaise (2 tablespoons)
  • Hot sauce or ranch dressing (2 tablespoons)
  • Olive oil (1 tablespoon, for frying )
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)

Steps

  1. Prepare the chicken tenders:
    • If using homemade, dip the chicken strips in flour, dip them in beaten eggs, and coat them in breadcrumbs or panko. Season with salt and pepper.
    • Heat olive oil in a frying pan over medium-high heat.
    • Fry the chicken tenders for 3-4 minutes on each side, until golden brown and crispy.
    • Remove the chicken from the pan and set aside to drain excess oil on a paper towel.
  2. Prepare the Wrap:
    • Lay a tortilla on a flat surface.
    • Spread mayonnaise and your choice of hot sauce or ranch dressing on the tortilla.
  3. Assemble the Wrap:
    • Place a few leaves of lettuce, tomato slices, cucumber slices, and cheese on the tortilla.
    • Lay the crispy fried chicken tenders on top of the veggies.
  4. Wrap it Up:
    • Fold the sides of the tortilla inwards and roll it up tightly, ensuring everything is snug inside.

Serve immediately and enjoy your crispy fried chicken wrap!
